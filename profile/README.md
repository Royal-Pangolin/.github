# :wave: Welcome to Our GitHub Organization

This organization is a shared space created by two Computer Engineering students from the [University Pablo de Olavide](https://www.upo.es/escuela-politecnica-superior/es/) (Spain).

## :dart: Purpose

We built this organization as a collaborative hub where we can:

- Store and manage group projects  
- Work together on assignments  
- Share ideas and experiments  
- Explore new technologies as a team  

It serves as a middle ground — a virtual workspace where collaboration and creativity come together.

## :handshake: Collaboration First

This is a place where teamwork matters. Every repository here may include:

- Joint contributions  
- Individual contributions  
- Knowledge sharing  
- Continuous improvement  

We believe that working together — while also developing independently — leads to better solutions and deeper understanding.

## :books: Our Values

- **Learning above all**  
  We see every project as an opportunity to grow, experiment, and improve our skills.

- **Support for Free Software**  
  We value openness, transparency, and the principles of free and open-source software.

## :rocket: What You'll Find Here

- Academic projects  
- Collaborative assignments  
- Individual and team-based work  
- Side projects and prototypes  
- Ideas we find interesting and worth exploring  

## :seedling: Always Evolving

This organization is a living space. As we learn and grow, so will the projects and ideas shared here.
